Why not just go straight to a broker?

Not all brokers are the same — and that matters

Some brokers handle simple cases only. If your income is complex, your credit isn't perfect, or your deposit is non-standard, a generalist may not be the right first call.

Others specialise in complex situations. Self-employed income, adverse credit, unusual deposit sources — different brokers have different lender relationships and access.

Speaking to the wrong broker first can lead to wasted time — or being told something isn't possible when it actually is.

We look at your situation first and point you to someone suited to it.

Before you speak to anyone, we assess your income, deposit and credit profile against what lenders actually look for. That means the conversation you have next is with the right person — not just the nearest one.
