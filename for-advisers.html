Why work with BorrowCheck

Better leads. Better conversion. Less wasted time.

🎯

Pre-qualified leads only

Every client that reaches you has already filled in a detailed assessment. You know their mortgage type, deposit, employment status, credit profile, and any complexity flags before you make first contact.

🔗

Matched to your specialism

We match clients to advisers based on declared case-type experience. If you specialise in self-employed or adverse credit cases, you'll get clients that match — not random enquiries from unrelated case types.

📈

Higher conversion rates

Because clients are matched to you specifically, and have already been assessed before contacting you, they arrive with higher intent and more realistic expectations — leading to better conversion.

⏱️

Less time on unsuitable cases

The assessment process filters out unsuitable clients before they reach you. You spend more of your time on cases you can actually help with — and less time on initial calls that go nowhere.

🛡️

Clients who understand the process

BorrowCheck educates clients throughout the assessment. By the time they speak to you, they understand how lenders assess them — which means better-informed conversations and faster progress.

🤝

A partnership, not a transaction

We work with a carefully maintained network of advisers. We're not a bulk lead provider. We expect quality outcomes from both sides — and our advisers get ongoing matching based on performance.

How it works

Simple, straightforward process

1

Introductory call

We have a short call to understand your specialism, lender access, case history, and capacity. No commitment required.

2

Vetting & onboarding

We verify your FCA authorisation, review case history, and agree the case types you'll be matched to. Takes 3–5 working days.

3

Receive matched leads

We send you client enquiries that match your specialism, along with a pre-assessment summary so you're prepared before first contact.

4

Work the case, report outcomes

You work with the client in the normal way. We ask for brief outcome reporting, which feeds into ongoing matching quality.

Demonstrable experience in your stated specialisms

Client-first approach
